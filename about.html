About Us

Goral Art

Design is plan rearanging elements in such a way as best to accomplish a particular purpose. For an interior design to be successful, the object in it must communicate with one another respond and balance one another.



Goral Art is well known in Gujarat as well as some region of Indian Market as a manufacturer and exporter of premium quality products in FRP (Fibre Reinforced Plastic). The credit for this world class quality is result of the adoption of the latest technology available in the industry. After a modest beginning, a couple of years ago the company has grown rapidly.



Our Mission

We would like to be amongst the top most customized FRP products manufacturing companies in the country, providing premium products in terms of quality, price, precision and perfection. We also have expertise to develop any kind of new design from a photo given by customer as per theirs ideas and according to their requirment.

So whether you are looking to design a custom new home/offices or planning an addition or renovation, Goral Art can assist in helping you to produce your dream and decorative firm.

Our Vision

The company's vision is to live to the expectation of customers and provide a better solutions to potential customers through focues on technology upgradation and innovation.
